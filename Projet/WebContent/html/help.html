Login help:

As a new user for the PAJME online application, you should've received a user ID. The default testing ID is M001, you can use it to test out our application's capabilities. Below is an image showing what the login page looks like:
You only need to input your login credential in the highlighted text field and press the submit button to login to your PAJME user panel.

User page:

To modify your own personal information, click the highlighted link:

That will bring you to the modify personal information page, where you fill out the fields you want to modify and then press submit to confirm the changes.


To view a list of either, patients, doctors or consultations, click one of the highlighted buttons:



Patient list:

To add a new patient, just press the add patient button, it will bring you to a form and let you input a patient's information.


To view another doctor's patients, input his ID into the highlighted field and press the button.


Other than viewing the patient list, you can also see a patient's prescription, modify his information or delete his/her information from the system by choosing one of the highlighted options at the right end of the table and then pressing go.


If you're trying to modify a patient's information, this will be the page on which you'll be once you press go:
